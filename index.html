Spiritual Growth 

I had one of the best experiences last week! I was able to go to the Wilds with my Senior class. It was an amazing week becaus grew closer to God, closer to my classmates, and closer to my teachers. God really spoke to me through the preaching of God's Word. I was really convicted that I was taking God for granted and I wasn't thanking him for what he did for me.

My Senior Class

I am blessed with an awesome class. Our whole class grew closer together because of our friendships and because of who our God is. We went rafting, hiking, tubing, mini golfing, and played a lot of sports. These activities were a lot of fun, but most important of all, we grew closer spiritually. A lot of us made descisions to keep each other accountable in our walk with God. We left the Wilds wanting to make a difference in the lives of the younger classman. 

Teachers

God used a lot of the teachers to inspire me to grow closer to God. I got to relate to my principle how hard it was to really stay close to God. He replied and said that he struggled with the same things. It was an awesome experience to see how my principle wanted to help me to the point where he spend money to buy me a book that encouraged me to get closer to God. I'm so blessed to go to an amazing school!
